Ella's Room Design Ideas

Here are some ideas that I have for the computer science room on 14. I am going for a whimsical, yet modern look.

These egg chairs are super cool and would look perfect in this space. We could get multiple and they would be a great place for students to get work done. EGG CHAIRS

This desk would look great if a couple were lined up next to eachother. They are sleek and very modern, as well as being really useful. We could put desktops on them so that while having a lab, teachers can sit at these desks and work with students Floating Desk

A couple of these hanging plants will really brighten up the room. These hanging planters can be filled with fake plants (or real ones if someone would be responsible for watering it) Hanging planter

A couple of these beanbags will make the room really fun! They will encourage people to come and hang out and code in this room. beanbag

These fairy lights are super cheap and will make the room whimsical and pretty! Just one order of them comes with 10 ft of lights, so we can put them all around the ceiling of the room! fairy lights

A couple of these hammocks will make great places to sit while coding. They stick with the modern/whimsical theme and they are super cool hammock chairs
